Controlled copper process

From incoming material to cast ingots — a clear, transparent flow designed for quality and traceability.

  1. 01

    Intake & sorting

    Copper-bearing material is received, visually inspected and sorted by type and quality, with non-conforming fractions removed.

  2. 02

    Remelting

    Sorted material is remelted in controlled furnaces. Temperature, slag removal and alloying are managed according to defined parameters.

  3. 03

    Refining & sampling

    Before casting, samples are taken for analysis. Parameters such as copper content and impurities are checked to meet agreed specs.

  4. 04

    Ingot casting & marking

    Molten copper is cast into standardized ingots. Each batch is marked and linked to laboratory and production records.
